CVR College of Engineering - Administration and Academic Leadership

Director

Prof. K. Rama Sastri
Qualifications:
- B.E.
- M.E.
- Ph.D. (IIT Madras)

Profile:
Prof. K. Rama Sastri served as Rector of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) until July 2002. Throughout his academic career, he held positions including Professor, Head of Department, Vice Principal, Principal, Director, and Registrar at JNTU.

Achievements:
- Published over 25 research papers in national and international journals and conferences.
- Guided more than 30 M.Tech projects.
- Recipient of the Andhra Pradesh State Government Best Teacher Award (University Teacher Category, 1994).

Contribution to CVR:
- Served as Principal of CVR College of Engineering from 2002.
- Played a key role in transforming CVR into one of the leading engineering colleges in Telangana.
- Appointed Director of CVR College of Engineering in 2008.
