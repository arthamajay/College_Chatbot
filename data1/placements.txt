CVR College of Engineering - Placement Statistics

PLACEMENTS 2025-26

Overview:
The 2025-26 placement season continued CVR's strong placement record despite a challenging hiring market. More than 90 companies visited the campus and over 1250 students secured placements.

Key Statistics:
- Total Placements: 1251+
- Companies Visited: 90+
- Highest Package: Rs. 55 LPA
- Offers Above Rs. 6 LPA: 375+
- High Salary Offers: 175+

Top Recruiters:
- Google
- Amazon
- ServiceNow
- Commvault
- JP Morgan
- Oracle
- Walmart
- Infosys
- TCS
- Accenture
- Cognizant
- Deloitte
- PwC

Highest Packages:
- Google – Rs. 55 LPA
- ServiceNow – Rs. 46.32 LPA
- Amazon Future Engineer – Rs. 42 LPA
- Commvault – Rs. 32.99 LPA
- Trilogy Innovations – Rs. 32 LPA

Highlights:
- One of the highest placement counts among peer colleges.
- Strong presence of product-based companies.
- Significant number of offers from multinational corporations.
- Majority of students placed in IT and Core Engineering sectors.
