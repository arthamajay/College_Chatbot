Transportation Facilities

Overview

CVR College of Engineering operates a dedicated fleet of college buses connecting various locations across Hyderabad and Secunderabad to the college campus.

Transportation Features

- More than 50 bus routes.
- Coverage across Hyderabad and Secunderabad.
- Dedicated transportation support team.
- Direct connectivity to major residential areas.
- Daily pickup and drop facilities.
