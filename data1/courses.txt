Minor Degree Programs

Available Minor Programs:
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI&ML)
- Cyber Security (CS)
- Data Science (DS)
- Internet of Things (IoT)

Eligibility:
- Available to B.Tech students from the third year onwards.
- Students must complete 160 credits in the major program and 18 additional credits to earn a Minor Degree.
- Students enrolled in AI&ML, Cyber Security, or Data Science majors may choose another minor specialization.
